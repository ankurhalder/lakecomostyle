Dee Lasprogata

Founder & Creative Director

Our founder has over 30 years of experience in fashion, working in New York, Los Angeles, Rome, Milan, and Lake Como. She arrived to the shores of Lake Como 20 years ago and has been back and forth ever since.

With roots in Philadelphia, Dee grew up surrounded by couture as her grandmother operated a custom gown atelier. She graduated from both Vanderbilt University and the Fashion Institute of Design in New York City.

She has launched pioneering fashion collections working with celebrities including Valentino, Stevie Wonder and Giada De Laurentiis. Her visionary work has been featured in major publications including People, Glamour, Io Donna, Women's Wear Daily (WWD), Fox & Friends, TG5, and Entrepreneur.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are there weekly events open to all guests visiting Lake Como?

Yes! We host weekly events for all guests visiting Lake Como, ranging from casual aperitivi to pre-fixed dinners at select lakeside locations. Each week offers a unique experience, allowing guests to enjoy the best of local cuisine, stunning views, and the vibrant atmosphere of the Lake.

Can I book a private event for a bachelorette, rehearsal dinner or tour group gathering?

Yes! We offer private events tailored for bachelorettes, tour groups, corporate functions, and other special gatherings. Whether it's a curated aperitivo, a pre-fixed dinner, or a full lakeside experience, we can customize the event to suit your group's needs and create a memorable experience on Lake Como.

What backdrops and props do you offer?

We offer a collection of over 3,000 couture costumes and props, spanning multiple time periods, styles, and themes. The collection includes elegant, theatrical, historical, fantasy, and bespoke pieces, each selected for its craftsmanship, visual impact, and suitability for immersive, high-end events.

How long in advance do I need to book a private event?

We recommend booking private events at least 2‚Äì4 weeks in advance to ensure availability and allow us time to tailor the experience to your group. For larger groups or peak season dates, we suggest booking as early as possible to secure your preferred date and location on Lake Como.

Do you offer custom themes?

Absolutely! While we have signature themes like La Dolce Vita, Agents of Style, and Hollywood Hustle, we specialize in creating fully customized themes tailored to your vision, brand, or celebration. Our team works closely with you to design every detail.

Can guests participate in the filming?

Yes! Guests can choose their level of participation. Some prefer to take on starring roles and fully immerse themselves in the performances, while others may simply observe and enjoy the narrative as it unfolds. Our professional actors can enact the full story, ensuring everyone feels comfortable and enjoys the experience.

What is included in the movie trailer package?

The movie trailer package includes professional filming, a fully edited 3-minute cinematic trailer with custom title, cast names, and your choice of music, plus social media-ready clips. The trailer is delivered within 3-4 weeks of your event.
